Cookies and local storage

Last updated: 17 June 2026

Current use

The site uses browser local storage for necessary preferences. Google Analytics 4 is activated only after you select “Accept” in the cookie preferences. No advertising cookies are used by Markellos Ecosystem.

Values stored locally

The site may store the following values in your browser:

  • Consent preference: remembers whether you selected necessary-only storage or accepted analytics, so the preference banner does not appear on every visit.
  • Interface settings: remembers choices such as colour theme, text size, title layout, circle size, spacing, font, background detail and motion preference.
  • Circle layout: stores saved circle positions when the layout-management functionality is used.

Optional analytics

After you select “Accept”, Google Analytics may store analytics information in your browser to measure visits and interactions. Selecting “Necessary only” keeps Google Analytics disabled.

Managing stored preferences

Use the Cookie preferences control on the home page to change your choice. You can also clear the stored site data in your browser to restore the default settings.

External websites

Each linked blog, application or website may use its own cookies and local storage. Please consult the information provided by the relevant external website.
