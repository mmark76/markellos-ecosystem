Privacy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Information collected by this site

Markellos Ecosystem is a directory of independently hosted blogs, applications and websites. The landing page does not currently include account registration, contact forms, advertising trackers or analytics tools.

Information stored in your browser

The site uses local storage in your browser to remember your consent preference, interface settings and saved circle positions. These values remain on your device and are used to restore your chosen appearance and layout on later visits.

Local storage is not automatically sent with web requests. You can remove these values by clearing the stored site data for Markellos Ecosystem in your browser, after which the site returns to its default settings and the preference banner may appear again.

Technical hosting data

Hosting and network providers may process standard technical information required to deliver and protect the website, such as IP addresses, request times and browser data.

External websites

Links in the ecosystem open separate websites. Their own privacy policies and data practices apply once you leave this site.
